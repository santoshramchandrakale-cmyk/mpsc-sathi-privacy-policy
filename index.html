Privacy Policy for MPSC SATHI

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information MPSC SATHI may collect, why it is used, and what choices you have while using the app.

1. Information we may collect

When you use MPSC SATHI, we may collect basic account details such as your name, email address, and profile photo if you sign in with Google. We may also store your study progress, completed tests, saved questions, scores, rankings, preferences, and subscription status. This helps the app give you a more personalized and continuous experience.

2. How your information is used

We use your information to help you sign in securely, save your progress, show your performance, restore your purchased premium access, and make sure your app experience continues smoothly across sessions. Your information may also be used to improve app quality, fix problems, and understand which features are useful for users.

3. Login and account access

MPSC SATHI currently uses Google Sign-In for account access. This allows you to log in securely without creating a separate password inside the app. The app may receive basic profile details from your Google account only for login and account-related purposes.

4. Study data and app activity

To support learning features, the app may save information such as your practice history, selected answers, scores, rankings, bookmarks, and test-related activity. This is used to help you track your preparation, continue where you left off, and view your strengths and weaker areas more clearly.

5. Ads and premium features

Free users may see advertisements in the app. These ads help support the free version of MPSC SATHI. If you purchase premium through Google Play, the app checks your subscription status so ads can be removed and premium features can be unlocked. We do not ask you to share payment card details directly inside the app, because payments are handled through Google Play.

6. Notifications

The app may send notifications related to important updates, reminders, or useful app activity. You can manage notification permissions through your device settings at any time.

7. Offline content and downloaded material

Some content may be available directly inside the app without internet. In some cases, additional question content or related study material may be downloaded and stored on your device so you can continue using it in the app more conveniently.

8. Analytics and technical improvements

We may use trusted services from Google and Firebase to understand app usage, improve performance, fix crashes, and keep the app working properly. This helps us identify technical issues and improve the overall user experience.

9. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Some data may be processed through trusted third-party services used for login, app functionality, analytics, notifications, advertising, and subscription verification. These services are used only to support the normal working of the app.

10. Your choices and control

You can sign out of the app at any time. You can also manage app permissions, notification access, ad-related settings, and your subscription through your device settings or Google Play account settings. If you no longer want to use premium, you can cancel your subscription through Google Play.

11. Data security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and protect it from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure in every situation.

12. Children’s privacy

MPSC SATHI is intended for learners and exam preparation users. If required by law or platform policy, parents or guardians should supervise younger users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable rules.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the app changes, new features are added, or legal requirements are updated. The latest version will always be available inside the app and on this public page.
